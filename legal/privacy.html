Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

ReviveAI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ReviveAI (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Photos and Images: When you use the enhancement features, you upload photos to the App. These images are processed locally on your device for preview and then sent to third-party AI services (see Section 3) for enhancement.
  • Replicate API Key: You may voluntarily provide your personal Replicate.com API token in the Settings. This key is stored only locally on your device using secure device storage (SharedPreferences on Android / Keychain on iOS). We do not send your API key to our servers.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (device model, operating system version, app version) for analytics and crash reporting (if enabled in future updates).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as which enhancement features you use most, number of enhancements performed, and crash reports to improve the App.
  • Local History: Enhanced photos and metadata (enhancement type, date) are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account information (the App does not require login or registration).
  • Precise location data.
  • Advertising identifiers for tracking unless you explicitly opt in for future features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the core functionality of the App (photo enhancement and restoration).
  • Process your images using AI models via third-party services.
  • Improve the App, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse.
  • Communicate with you (if you contact support).

We do not sell your personal information or photos.

3. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

The App relies on the following third-party services to function:

3.1 Replicate (Primary AI Processing)

  • Your uploaded images are sent directly from your device to Replicate, Inc. (https://replicate.com) for AI processing.
  • Replicate receives the image content and returns the enhanced result.
  • Replicate may temporarily store images for processing and logging purposes. Their use of your data is governed by Replicate’s own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
  • Important: We have no control over how Replicate handles your images after they are transmitted. You should review Replicate’s policies before using the App.

3.2 Other Services

  • Flutter / Google / Apple: Standard platform services for app delivery, crash reporting, and in-app purchases.
  • Share functionality: When you share a photo, it uses your device’s native sharing capabilities (no data is sent through our servers).

We do not share your photos or API keys with any other parties except as described above.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All photos, history, and your Replicate API key are stored exclusively on your device. Deleting the App or clearing app data will permanently remove this information from your device.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your photos, enhanced results, or personal data on our servers.
  • Security: We use industry-standard practices to protect the App. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Access & Deletion: You can view and delete your enhancement history directly in the App at any time (History tab → delete individual items or clear all).
  • API Key: You can remove your Replicate API key in Settings at any time.
  • Opt-out: You may stop using the App at any time. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.
  • Data Portability: Your enhanced photos are saved as standard image files on your device and can be accessed via your device’s file manager.

If you have questions about your data, contact us at the email below.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

7. International Users

The App is operated from Pakistan. If you are located outside Pakistan, please be aware that your information (including photos) may be transferred to and processed in the United States (via Replicate) and other countries. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.
